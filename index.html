    Introduction About "MEGA Talks"

    The Mega Talks, a program sponsored by HCL Technologies to gather experience-based insights among several senior technology executives on essential topics. At HCL, we believe sharing experiences and knowledge is the key to building a better world today, which can thrive and sustain tomorrow. Each business is on its unique path, and the people leading it can help others by sharing these unique experiences and solutions with the larger audience. Talks will focus on topics relevant to the industry.
