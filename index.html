The MEGA Talks

An experience-based insights among several senior technology executives on essential topics. At HCL, we believe sharing experiences and knowledge is the key to building a better world today, which can thrive and sustain tomorrow. The discussion will focus on topics relevant to the industry and technology landscape. Insights will be shared to tackle current challenges, assess the pros & cons of different strategies, and gain actionable advice – all while raising your thought leadership profile. MEGA Talks in collaboration with HCL, looks forward to sharing the experiences to inspire the world that no matter how hard yesterday hit us or how rough today is, the zest to build better tomorrow always prevails. Together, we will have an #InspireTomorrow.
